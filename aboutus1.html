Our Story



The Wine House has been serving customers from around the world for over 40 years from our 18,000 square foot facility in West Los Angeles, CA.

We provide a unique, exciting shopping environment for clients ranging in wine experience from the complete novice to the connoisseur. Our vast selection of over 6,000 domestic and imported wines, spirits and beers is one of the largest and well-curated in the country. We are honored to be the First Call for customers seeking products or assistance with any of our wide variety of products





WINES

When you need help making your wine selection, our nationally recognized staff of experts is ready to help. Our team of highly experienced and knowledgeable wine professionals will assist you in choosing the right product for any occasion.

PREMIUMSPIRITS

Whether stocking your ‘back bar’ or ‘well’, we can take care of it all. Be it the ﬁnest Cognacs and Brandies, select Bourbons, specialty Tequilas or the best in Single Malt Scotches, we carry a large selection of products from the most obscure producers to the most well known national brands.

BEERS

We sell a wide variety of beers and ales, both imported and domestic, from major brands as well as micro brews. Whether you are looking for a Belgian Triple or a local craft IPS, we’ve got you covered.

WINE EDUCATION

Wine House classes are designed to let you take advantage of the extensive wealth of knowledge gleaned by our staff of instructors through a combined 80 years as wine industry professionals. We schedule 43 weeks of fun and informative classes every year.


WINE ACCESSORIES

From a basic wine opener to the latest high-tech device, we have a full complement of useful items. We carry an array of wine glasses and decanters, wine books, wine racks of all sizes, temperature control totes and other unique items.

WSET

For individuals new to the subject, this qualification offers a hands-on introduction to the world of spirits. You’ll explore the main styles and types of spirits through sight, smell, and taste to develop an understanding of the key factors affecting flavors and aromas.
